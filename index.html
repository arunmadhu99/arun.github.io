FIRST GLOBAL COLLOQUIUM ON RECENT ADVANCEMENT AND EFFECTUAL RESEARCHES IN ENGINEERING, SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY

 'RAEREST'

on 22nd and 23rd april, 2016

at St. Joseph's College Of Engineering And  Technology,

Palai, Kottayam, Kerala, India

The international Conference on Global Colloquium in Recent Advancement and Effectual Researches in Engineering Science and Technology (RAEREST) focus on providing an international opportunity for sharing the ideas, knowledge and information of eminent and experienced professionals from industries, research organizations, faculty, research scholars and students from academic institutions among experts and researchers working in their area of interest.

 

The theme of the conference is Human Empowerment through Technology. The program of the conference constitutes plenary lectures, keynote lectures and invited talks from eminent scientists/technologists/ industry professionals working in the area of Mechanical, Civil, Computer Science, Electrical engineering, Electronics Engineering, Applied Electronics Engineering and related applications

 

Download brochure

Reviewed and accepted papers will be published as one dedicated issue in Elsevier's Procedia Technology Journal (ISSN: 2212-0173) and made available in open-access on Elsevier's Science Direct, which is used by millions of researchers and professionals on a monthly basis guaranteeing maximum visibility of the research paper.

 

For more information about the journal, please visit :

GUEST OF HONOUR

Dr. George Ofori

Professor at the Department of Building, National University of Singapore

He is a Fellow of the Ghana Academy of Arts and Sciences. He is also a fellow of the Chartered Institute of Building, Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors, and Society of Project Managers (Singapore). He was a senior quantity surveyor with the largest cost consultancy firm in Ghana. He was the Head of the Department of Building at NUS in 2002-07. He is currently the Director of the multi-disciplinary M.Sc.(Environmental Management) program. He has taught courses at undergraduate and graduate levels on Construction Economics, Construction Management, Project Management, Environmental Management and Sustainable Development. His research is on construction industry development (especially in developing countries), international project management, sustainability in construction, and leadership development in construction. He has been a consultant to several international agencies, and governments on construction industry development. He was the Founding Coordinator of the Working Group 107 on Construction in Developing Countries of the International Council for Research and Innovation in Building and Construction (CIB). He is now the Deputy Chairman of the International Board of Construction Sector Transparency Initiative (CoST).

The conference is held at St. Josephs college of Engineering and Technology, Choondacherry, Pala. It is one among the top rated engineering colleges in Kerala. The college is established in 2002, managed by the diocese of Palai and is affiliated to Mahathma Gandhi University, Kottayam. SJCET offers courses like under graduate courses in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Applied and Electronics Engineering and Post graduate courses in all the above branches. SJCET also offers Masters in Computer Application and Business Administration.

 

The central library of SJCET is one of the biggest college libraries in Kerala with 15000 square feet area. The library owns more than 35000 books, 15000 online journals, 1700 e books, 17 new papers and other resources like question papers, thesis reports, digital resources etc. each and every departments own a department library with more than 1500 books. Every research oriented faculties are giving a separate cubicle in the library for helping them in their research work.

 

SJCET provides an echo friendly green campus with well-equipped infrastructure. The labs and classes are constantly innovated and upgraded with most advanced equipment. The team of faculty members is highly committed and has an admirable blend of experience. The hostels of SJCET are well furnished and the college owns an organic farm to provide the students with fresh food.

 

SJCET has started BootCamp to promote entrepreneurship among students, with the financial support of Startup Village. SJCET has obtained NBA accreditation and ISO 9001: 2008 certifications.

 

SJCET has a very vibrant placement cell and around 250 students have got placement in the year 2015.

The conference is held at St. Josephs college of Engineering and Technology, Choondacherry, Pala. It is one among the top rated engineering colleges in Kerala. The college is established in 2002, managed by the diocese of Palai and is affiliated to Mahathma Gandhi University, Kottayam. SJCET offers courses like under graduate courses in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering and Applied and Electronics Engineering and Post graduate courses in all the above branches. SJCET also offers Masters in Computer Application and Business Administration.

 

The central library of SJCET is one of the biggest college libraries in Kerala with 15000 square feet area. The library owns more than 35000 books, 15000 online journals, 1700 e books, 17 new papers and other resources like question papers, thesis reports, digital resources etc. each and every departments own a department library with more than 1500 books. Every research oriented faculties are giving a separate cubicle in the library for helping them in their research work.

 

SJCET provides an echo friendly green campus with well-equipped infrastructure. The labs and classes are constantly innovated and upgraded with most advanced equipment. The team of faculty members is highly committed and has an admirable blend of experience. The hostels of SJCET are well furnished and the college owns an organic farm to provide the students with fresh food.

 

SJCET has started BootCamp to promote entrepreneurship among students, with the financial support of Startup Village. SJCET has obtained NBA accreditation and ISO 9001: 2008 certifications.

 

SJCET has a very vibrant placement cell and around 250 students have got placement in the year 2015.
